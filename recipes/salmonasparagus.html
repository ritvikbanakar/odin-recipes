One Pan Lemon Garlic Salmon and Asparagus

Description

Healthy, Quick, and Flavorful.

This one pan lemon-garlic salmon and asparagus is packed with the delicious flavors of lemon-garlic and butter.

All Credits to Layla from Gimme Delicious

Total Time: 10 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 pound salmon fillet cut into 2-3 fillets
  • 1 bunch asparagus trimmed
  • 1 tablespoon salted butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • Zest and juice of 1/2 lemon
  • Salt and cracked black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a tablespoon of butter and a tablespoon of olive oil in a large pan.
  2. Once pan is hot and butter has melted, add salmon and asparagus.
  3. Season with salt and pepper, then cook for about 3-4 minutes on one side
  4. Flip, then cook other side for 3-4 minutes as well.
  5. Add garlic and lemon zest.
  6. Cook the garlic for just 1-2 minutes or until it begins to turn brown.
  7. Turn off the heat and squeeze half a lemon into the dish
  8. Eat up!
